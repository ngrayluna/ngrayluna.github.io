Why Did I Create This?

Background on what inspired me to create a site.

September 27, 2017

Greetings from Somewhere in Germany,

Disclaimer[1]: This "site" is under mainatanence. I did something quick and dirty using Bootstrap to get something up fast. My plan is to make the site more robust which involves putthing this under Jekyll control at some point.

Disclaimer[2]: I like long walks on the beach, roses, strawberries dipped in chocolate...I'm kidding. Sand gets everywhere and roses die quickly. I do love chocolate though...

OK, I've now wasted probably a healthy minute of your time. My goal is complete.

I am creating this blog as a way to keep some folks up-to-date while I am away from the West Coast. It should be noted that I do not think my life is particularly interesting. In fact, I have some reservations about making a personal website. I do not want to come off as a narcissist and/or give off the impression I am full of myself.

The purpose of this blog is this: while I am away I want to be able to share my life with those close to me. I hope this platform of communication can: keep folks "in the loop" with what I am up to, provide insight to University life (besides being poor and developing unhealthy habits *laughsobs*), things that stand out to me as a native Californian living in Germany, and whatever seems worth writing about Germany. (I might provide information on research-related topics as well when that starts rolling around.)

Honestly, I would rather have these conversations with you all in person over lunch, baking, long walks on the beach, etc., but I understand that time zone differences, in addition to that fact that you all have your own lives to live, will make that difficult for a while.

That being said, two years will go by fast. I am looking forward to seeing you all again soon.

Sincerely,
N. Grayson
