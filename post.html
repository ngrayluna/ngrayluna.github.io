First Steps in a Foreign Land

I have no idea what I am doing. And I am OK with that.

Posted by Me on September 27, 2017

Greetings from Stanberg, Germany

Disclaimer[1]: This "site" is under mainatanence. I did something quick and dirty using Bootstrap, but I plan to use Jekyll in the future to make this site more robust.

Disclaimer[2]: I like long walks on the beach, roses, strawberries dipped in chocolate...I'm kidding. Sand gets everywhere and roses die quickly. I do love chocolate though...

OK, I've now wasted probably a healthy minute of your time. My goal is complete.

I am creating this blog as a way to keep some folks up-to-date on what I am doing while I am in Germany. I am not going to pretend that my life is particularly interesting. In fact, I have some reservations about making this as I don't want to come off as a narcissist and/or make people feel as if my life is worth blogging about.

The purpose of this blog is this: While I am away I want to be able to share my life with those close to me. I hope this platform can: keep folks "in the loop" with what I am up to, inform future interested parties of University life (besides being poor and picking up unhealthy habits *laughsobs*) in Germany for "higher education",things that stand out to me as a Californian living in Germany, and whatever seems worth writing about. Ideally, I would have these conversations with you all in person over lunch, baking, long walks on the beach, etc., but I understand that the time zone difference, in addition to that fact that you all have your own lives to live, will make that difficult.

That being said, two years will go by fast. I am looking forward to seeing you all again soon saying absurd things in person.

Sincerely,
N. Grayson
