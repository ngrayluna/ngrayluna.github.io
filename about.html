Why A Website?

Background on what inspired me to create a site.

Greetings from Germany,

You: ”Why did you create a website? Seems a bit excessive, no?”

Yes and no. The idea of sharing what I am doing in my life via the interwebs is something I have mixed feelings about. I am a moderately closed person who does not fancy making themselves the center of attention.

That being said, I can not imagine maintaining friendships if I am not able to listen and/or share life experiences, thoughts, feelings, etc. with you all. (An arguably necessary component of maintaining a friendship.) Sadly, my current location will make that difficult for a while for both sides.

This blog is for you. I want to share my life with you all while an ocean stands in between. I hope this platform of communication can keep folks "in the loop" with what I am up to.

To shift the focus off myself I will also explore and try to provide: insight to University life (besides being poor and developing unhealthy habits *laughsobs*) in Germany, information on cultural differences between Californians and Bavarians, research I am working on, and whatever seems worth writing about.

Honestly, I would rather have these conversations with you all in person over lunch, baking, long walks on the beach, etc., but I understand that time zone differences, in addition to that fact that you all have your own lives to live, will make that difficult for a while.

Two years will go by fast. I look forward to seeing you all again soon.

Sincerely,
Grayson
